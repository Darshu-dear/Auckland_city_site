Places to Visit

Sky tower

Auckland war memorial museum

Auckland zoo

What you can do at the sky tower

  • View the city from 186 metres high above street level.
  • Panoramic 360˚ views up to 80 kilometres in every direction.
  • Spot key landmarks, the 53 volcanoes and other historical locations within the greater Tāmaki Makaurau area.

What you can do at Auckland museum

  • Immerse yourself in New Zealand’s indigenous culture.
  • Wander through exhibits showcasing New Zealand’s unique wildlifes.
  • Learn about New Zealand’s involvement in global conflicts.

What you can do at Auckland zoo

  • Participate in daily talks and feedings led by zookeepers
  • Experience native New Zealand wildlife up close
  • Watch spider monkeys, orangutans, and other primates swing through the trees in this lively and interactive habitat.
